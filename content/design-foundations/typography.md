#### Font Size Hierarchy
- Headers: 32px/24px/20px
- Body: 16px
- Small text: 14px
- Micro text: 12px
<!-- end-card -->

<!-- card {"variant": "warning", "tags": ["Accessibility", "WCAG"]} -->
#### Accessibility Guidelines
- Maintain minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1
- Use relative units (rem) for scalability
- Avoid all-caps for body text
